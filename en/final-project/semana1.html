1. Gestión de proyectos.

"Proyecto de Fabricación Digital" se presenta como una metodología para el desarrollo de la innovación centrada en las personas, ofreciendo una lente a través del cual se pueden ver los desafíos, y, finalmente, solucionarlas.

1. The project proposal for the Digital Manufacturing Project is human-oriented, feasible and functional. And be present involved.

Title:

Key finder with push button.

Issue:

We constantly forget where we left the keys when we left the house because of an oversight we have and we take time to find them and if it is too late to leave it generates a big problem.

Solution:

Adapting to our keys a keychain with an RF system with a novel design, we will find our keys since they will produce a sound, which will make it easier to locate the keys in our home or workplace.

General purpose:

Facilitate the location of our keys.

Specific goal:

• Create a keychain model.

• Adapt an RF system to the key ring.

• Design the push button.

2.-The proposal is of high social, economic impact.

Hypothesis:

We consider that many people are "forgetful", we found this product striking and useful. Thus we expect a great reception for our product dedicated to the general public.

3. The work team has the skills to develop the project.

• Each member of the team took the occupational health and safety course.

• With basic knowledge of programming and design, we will be able to create the product.

• Different surveys were carried out to verify if the problem actually exists and how it could be solved.

• Through different product designs, the best one was searched for its easy use by users.

4. The proposal is based on the reality and opportunities for innovation within the region.

• There is a large number of people who would help with this product surveys that will determine the reception of the product in the market.

• We have a large number of educational centers with inadequate seats for students since a large budget is not allocated to the subject of ergonomics because this problem affects several users.

• The first tests will be done in TECSUP to analyze the results and thus go out to the market.
